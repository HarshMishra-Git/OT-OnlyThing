# OT-OnlyThing - Black & White Design Theme Implementation

## Overview
Successfully transformed the e-commerce platform from a colorful blue/orange theme to a premium **BLACK & WHITE** design with strategic accent colors.

## Design Philosophy Achieved

### Primary Colors
- **Black (#000000)**: Hero sections, footer, primary buttons, text
- **White (#FFFFFF)**: Page backgrounds, cards, secondary buttons
- **Grays (#111827 - #F9FAFB)**: Borders, subtle backgrounds, secondary text

### Accent Colors (Strategic Use)
- **Green (#10B981)**: Primary CTAs ("Add to Cart", "Subscribe")
- **Red**: Error states, discount badges
- **Yellow**: Warning states
- **Gold**: Rating stars

## Key Changes

### Components
1. **Button Component**
   - Primary: Black background, white text
   - Secondary: White background with border
   - CTA: Vibrant green for shopping actions (NEW)
   - Outline: Black border with hover effect

2. **Header/Navigation**
   - White background
   - Black logo text
   - Clean, minimal design
   - Accent green cart badge

3. **Footer**
   - Black background
   - White text
   - Subtle gray accents

#### Homepage

#### Shop Page

#### Product Detail Page
